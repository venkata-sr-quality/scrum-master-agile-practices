# Daily Standup – Effective Format

## Purpose of Daily Standup
The Daily Standup is for:
- Inspecting progress toward Sprint Goal
- Identifying impediments
- Re-aligning the team

It is **not a reporting meeting**.

## Format (Sprint Goal–Centric)
Each team member answers:
1. What progress did I make toward the Sprint Goal?
2. What will I work on today?
3. Is there anything blocking or slowing me down?

## Rules
- Time-boxed (15 minutes)
- Problem solving happens after the standup
- Team talks to each other, not to Scrum Master

## Scrum Master Role
- Keep the conversation focused
- Identify blockers
- Follow up offline

## Scrum Master Insight
A good standup reveals problems early.
A bad standup hides them politely.
